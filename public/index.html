What is the FASTstack?

The FASTstack empowers developers to quickly iterate and launch at scale.

It's is a technology and language-agnostic approach to building modern, full-featured web apps, sites, and APIs. It empowers individuals and teams of all sizes to prototype, ship rapidly, and scale their apps in a secure, reproducible way.
